IBEX SPORT CLIMBING

About us

IBEX Sport Wall Climbing has been waiting for climbing lovers in Győr since 2014. Our goal is to make wall climbing accessible to everyone, from beginners to professional competitors. Since 2019, our hall has been the largest climbing hall in Dunántúl, offering the opportunity to experience the experience of climbing on 750 m2.

Boulder wall

It enables the practice of technical climbing without ropes and harnesses, enhancing the development of strength and movement coordination.

Child wall

It provides a safe and fun environment for children to develop their motor skills and confidence.

Gym

It is an excellent place to improve general fitness and endurance, supporting the strengthening of the muscle groups needed during climbing.

Campus wall

Ideal for practicing dynamic and explosive movements that increase finger and upper body strength as well as climbers' technical skills.
